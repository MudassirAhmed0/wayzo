FAQ

USER MANUAL

Parking

For your safety and the safety of others, please make sure you park in designated areas.


When using WAYZ, please park your E-scooter or E-bike outside of the main pedestrian Walkway and inside wayz parking zones only.

Getting the ride started

To start your scooter ride, place one foot firmly on the baseboard and use your other foot to push off against the ground a few times. Once you’ve gained some speed, press on the throttle near your right thumb to accelerate. Familiarize yourself with the throttle and squeeze the hand brake to slow down when needed.

Unlocking

Once you have located a WAYZ E-scooter or E-bike , unlock it by scanning the QR code. If you encounter difficulties scanning the QR code, turn on your flashlight while scanning.


If the app prompts an error code or “under maintenance” message, that E-scooter or E-bike is likely in-use or our team is on the way to retrieve it. Please try another nearby E-scooter or Ebike..

Pricing and Payment

Ride Charges

WAYZ charges a rate per minute as you ride for an E-scooter or E-bike and then. Rates and promotions may vary by city, day of the week, and time of day. Rides may also be subject to a minimum charge. Please refer to your WAYZ app for exact costs by tapping on an E-scooter or E-bike on the map or tapping “Scan to Ride” to see the rate.

Paying for the ride

Adding a Payment Method

Upon signing up with WAYZ, add a payment method to your account by navigating to your "Wallet" in the app menu. You can pay for each ride individually or add funds to your Wallet balance, which will be deducted after each ride.


Adding to your Wallet Balance

If you wish to add money to your balance, simply tap on "Wallet" in the WAYZ app menu, tap "Balance", and select the amount you wish to add. This will charge your default payment method.

Issues

Emergency

If you require immediate police or medical attention, please call your local emergency number once you and all involved parties are out of harm’s way, and the necessary authorities have been contacted, please email support@wayzco.com to reach out for WAYZ representative.

E-scooter or E-bike Issues

If you encounter any issues with a WAYZ E-scooter or E-bike that could affect your safety or ability to complete your ride, please stop riding it immediately, end your trip.

Trouble Locating E-scooter or E-bike

If you are unable to locate an E-scooter or E-bike that appears on the map, you can try the following:


  • Tap the E-scooter or E-bike on the map.
  • Tap “Ring” (or any other option that is available on ways) for the E-scooter or E-bike to emit a sound and ensure you are in an area with good reception. Search all open public spaces and check behind other E-scooter(s) or E-bike(s) and large structures. Please note that bad phone reception may interfere with locating an E-scooter or E-bike .

If you still cannot find the E-scooter or E-bike , submit a request to let us know.

Trouble Unlocking an E-scooter or E-bike

Once you have located a WAYZ on the map, unlock the E-scooter or E-bike either by scanning the QR code on the E-scooter or E-bike . If it is dark out, and you are continuing to have a hard time scanning the QR code, you can either enter it manually or you can turn on the flashlight while scanning. If the app prompts an “under maintenance” message then the E-scooter or E-bike is likely in use by others or under maintenance. Please try another nearby.


If you find a private lock on a WAYZ or the QR code is damaged. Please report the issue to customer service and try another E-scooter or E-bike .

Account

Creating An Account

You can create a WAYZ account using your phone number. If you sign up using a phone number, you will receive a text message with a verification code. Please enter the verification code to complete your registration process.

No verification message

If you signed up to ride with WAYZ using your phone number, you must be able to receive text messages on that number. Make sure you don’t have an SMS blocker on your device and that you are in an area with good reception. If you do not receive the verification message within one minute, you can request the code to be resent.

Closing your WAYZ account

Please submit a request and our customer support will assist you.

Promotions

WAYZ CREDITS

You can earn WAYZ credits by successfully inviting friends to ride who are new to WAYZ. You can do this by sharing your referral code.

The WAYZ credit amount is subject to change.

ADDING PROMO CODE

To add a (valid) promo code to your account, please go to your “Wallet” which can be found in the app sidebar. Then tap “Add Promo Code”. Once added, it will automatically be applied to your next ride. Please note that only ONE promo code can be applied to every ride, and the promo code with the closest expiration date will be applied first.

Safety

Legal age to ride

You must be the legal age to enter into binding contracts. For most countries, this means you need to be 16 years or older for scooters, and 16 years or older for our bikes. Before signing up for WAYZ, make sure to review the User Agreement for your country, which states age requirements.

Use of helmet

We strongly recommend that you wear a helmet every time you ride our bikes and scooters. The helmet should be Snell, CPSC, ANSI, or ASTM-approved. It should be sized, fitted, and fastened per the manufacturer's instructions.

We encourage riders to have two finger lengths between their chin and the strap to make sure the helmet is securely on their head. The helmet should be comfortable and snug on your head


  • It should not move around (for example, it should not fall to block your vision or fall behind your head, exposing your forehead). While helmet laws vary by region, country, and city, please always wear a helmet. Helmets and other protective gear are no guarantee against personal injury. If you choose not to wear a helmet and are injured, you chose to take that risk and we are not responsible.
